Jashwanth Reddy Baddam Light Opera

The Gondoliers

Act I – Scene: The Piazzetta, Venice

Scene.—The Piazzetta, Venice. The Ducal Palace on the right.

CHORUS.

Alas!

FIA.

Are four-and-twenty,
They, alas!

(During this chorus Antonio and the Gondoliers enter.)

FRANC.

Good morrow, pretty maids; for whom prepare ye These floral tributes extraordinary?
