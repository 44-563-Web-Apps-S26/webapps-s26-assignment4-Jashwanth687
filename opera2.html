The Mikado

Act I - Continued

KOK

SONG
And the banjo serenader, and the others of his race,
And the piano-organist - I've got him on the list!
And the person who can smile while writing "Kind regards,"
Then sends reminders marked in red across your calling cards.

POO

RECITATIVE
Your list appears to lengthen with remarkable speed.

KOK

SONG
And that pestilent photographer, forever in your way,
With a "just one more" insistence till the fading end of day;
And the clumsy social critic who declares all joy a crime,
Why, none of them would suffer if removed before their time!

POO

RECITATIVE
I see. A practical list, then, and carefully curated.
